Overview

This calculator computes a loan repayment schedule based on your inputs. It supports fixed-rate reducing balance loans with optional extra principal payments per period. Payment frequency options are quarterly, monthly, fortnightly, and weekly. An optional toggle allows the first instalment to be rounded down to the nearest $1,000. All calculations update automatically when inputs are changed.

How to Use

Step 1 — Enter loan parametersEdit the blue text inputs in the Loan Parameters panel. All fields recalculate the schedule instantly.
Step 2 — Review the summaryThe Payment Totals and Schedule Totals tables update automatically with every input change.
Step 3 — Review the scheduleScroll down to view the full payment-by-payment breakdown. The schedule terminates when the ending balance reaches zero.
Step 4 — Variable extra paymentsThe Extra Payment input applies the same fixed amount every period. The Excel version supports per-row editing for variable amounts per period.
Step 5 — Round 1st instalmentSet the toggle to Yes to round the period 1 ending balance down to the nearest $1,000. This increases the first payment to absorb the rounding difference. From period 2 onwards the schedule resumes from the rounded balance using the normal PMT amount.
Step 6 — PrintUse browser File > Print or Ctrl+P. Select landscape orientation and fit to page width for best results.

Scope — Supported Inputs

Loan amountAny positive dollar amount.
Annual interest rateBetween 0.01% and 50%. Enter as a percentage — e.g. 4.00 for 4.00%.
Loan period1 to 30 years (whole numbers only).
Payments per yearSelect from dropdown: 4 (quarterly), 12 (monthly), 26 (fortnightly), or 52 (weekly).
Start dateAny valid date. Payment dates are calculated forward from this date.
Extra paymentOptional fixed additional principal per period. Enter 0 for no extra payment.
Lender nameOptional reference text. Does not affect calculations.
Round 1st instalmentYes/No toggle. Yes rounds the period 1 ending balance down to the nearest $1,000 — common in Singapore housing loans.

Row Allocation Limits

Quarterly (4/year)120 periods max — supports full 30 years. No truncation risk.
Monthly (12/year)360 periods max — supports full 30 years. No truncation risk.
Fortnightly (26/year)Supports up to ~13 years 10 months. Beyond this the schedule may truncate.
Weekly (52/year)Supports up to ~6 years 11 months. Beyond this the schedule may truncate.
⚠ If you require fortnightly or weekly payments beyond these limits, use monthly payments as an approximation or request an extended version.

Calculation Method

Interest methodReducing balance. Interest per period = Annual Rate ÷ Payments per Year × Opening Balance.
Payment amountCalculated using the standard PMT formula. Rounded to 2 decimal places each period.
Extra payment capAutomatically capped so it does not exceed remaining principal on the final period.
Final periodThe last payment may be less than the scheduled amount when extra payments accelerate payoff.
RoundingAll monetary values are rounded to 2 decimal places at each step to prevent floating-point accumulation errors.
First instalment roundingWhen toggle is Yes: period 1 ending balance = FLOOR(Opening Balance − Interest, 1000). Principal = Opening Balance − Rounded Ending Balance. No extra payment applied in period 1.
Date calculationQuarterly/monthly payments step by exact calendar months. Fortnightly = 14-day intervals. Weekly = 7-day intervals. Public holidays and weekends are not adjusted.

Limitations — Not Supported

Variable interest rateFixed rate only. Variable or split rates are not supported.
Interest-only periodsAll payments include principal and interest from period 1.
Balloon paymentsLump sum final payments are not supported.
Offset accountsFunds in an offset account are not modelled.
Redraw facilitiesDrawing back extra payments already made is not reflected.
Daily compoundingUses simple per-period interest. May differ from lenders that compound daily.
Rule of 78sPre-computed interest schedules used by some lenders are not supported.
Fees and chargesEstablishment fees, monthly fees, or early repayment charges are not included.
Variable extra paymentsFixed extra payment only. The Excel version supports per-row variable amounts.

Disclaimer

This calculator is provided for indicative purposes only. Results may differ from your lender's actual loan statement due to differences in compounding method, rounding conventions, payment date adjustments, and fees not captured here. Always refer to your lender's official loan schedule and consult a qualified financial adviser before making financial decisions.
